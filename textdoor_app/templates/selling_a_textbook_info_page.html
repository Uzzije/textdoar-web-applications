How to Sell A Book

      Select the ‘List New Book’ option at the top of your screen
      Enter books information (Author, Title, ISBN etc.)
      When your book is bought, we will notify you via email, call, or text with options where you can leave your book based on your current living situation.
      We will send one of our Textdoar drivers out to your location to pick up your textbook, package it, and deliver it for you, including free shipping and handling.
      Two days after the textbook is picked up from your location we will deposit the payment into your bank account.
