Frequently Asked Questions

What is textdoar?


A textbook sharing marketplace that connects students with each other for the purchase and sell of textbooks locally


Q: Where did my book come from?


A: All books are listed from students here at Manhattan, Kansas.


Q: I just listed my book, what’s next?


A: The next step is to wait until someone buys your book. If your book is bought within 5 days after you list it, you receive a Chipotle Gift card.


Q: What do I do if I do not see the book I am looking for?


A: Enter the books information and we will notify you when it becomes available.


Q: How will I receive my Chipotle Gift card?


A: When we pick up your textbook that was bought, we will leave the Gift card in a sealed envelope with you or in your mailbox.


Q: I just got an email saying I sold my book, what do I do now?


A: Congratulations! You will be receiving a call or text shortly after to notify us when a convenient time is for you to have us pick up your book. That way you can relax and have us take care of everything else.


Q: How do I know my payments are secure?


A: All payments through textdoar go through Stripe, a financial technology company that allows both private individuals and businesses to accept payments over the Internet. They also handle payments for companies such as Lyft and Squarspace. We do not store your sensitive payment information on our server's either. This means that in the rare occasion that our secured systems get breached your information will be safe.


Q: I sold my book, it has been picked up, when do I receive my money?

A: All payments will be deposited into the sellers’ bank account two days after the book is picked up from the sellers’ house.


Q: Can anyone use this website?


A: No! Each buyer and seller must be a student here in Manhattan, Kansas or one who still has a valid edu email, i.e a recent graduate.


Q: When will this be at my University?


A: Soon! We are a young start-up company hoping to one day scale to every college in America. It is always goal to provide cheap and reliable textbooks to college students in the most convenient way possible across the nation. We are currently at Kansas State University but will be expanding to other campuses as we progress.
